About Me

I am a fan of editing and coding. With expertise in editing, I enjoy creating aesthetic and interesting visual works. On the other hand, my coding skills allow me to develop creative and functional solutions in various projects. This combination allows me to combine art and technology, creating works that are not only stunning but also effective. I am always challenged to continue learning and developing my skills in these two fields.

I am a high school student with a passion for the world of film. My best achievement was winning the FLS2N competition (National Student Arts Festival and Competition) in the short film category. I combine creativity, sophisticated editing techniques, and strong storytelling skills to create inspiring and riveting films. This victory is not only a matter of personal pride, but also motivates me to continue working and developing my skills in the film industry.
